CSCI 375 Spring 2020

Course Notes

All course materials will be posted here, including: lecture notes, labs, and project details. Course submissions will be done with git.

Course Outline: CSCI 375 Outline. Please read this outline, as it contains important information about this course.

Grades: VIU Learn

Recommended textbook: Systems Analysis and Design in a Changing World. 6th or 7th edition are both OK. In Readings, below, the 6th edition chapter and pages will be in parentheses ( ). This textbook will be supplemented with online resources as much as possible.

Students are responsible for all material presented in class, in assigned readings, in labs, and covered by the project. In class we will cover material and work through examples. Students are expected to attend all classes, and participate actively, as this is the best way to learn this material.
